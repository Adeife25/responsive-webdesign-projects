Recipe For Chinchin

Prep time

10 min

Cook time

20 min

Servings

4

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add the melted butter, milk, vanilla extract, and egg. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth.
  4. Roll out the dough to about 1/4 inch thickness and cut into small pieces.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the pieces until golden brown.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels. Serve warm or at room temperature.
